## What the app does
- Share cat photos with captions, tags, and optional location.
- Browse feeds, explore tags/collections, and follow other cat people.
- Create collections to organize favorite posts.
- Edit your profile (bio, location text, avatar) and manage your account.

## Getting started
- Create an account with email, username, and password.
- Log in to start posting and following.
- Guests can browse limited profiles/collections where allowed.

## Posting a cat
- Pick or capture a photo (camera or photo library).
- Add name/description and tags; optionally attach your current location.
- Tap post to upload; the image is stored securely and linked to your post.

## Collections
- Create a collection with a name/description.
- Save your posts to collections and manage cats within each collection.

## Following and engagement
- Follow/unfollow users; likes and follows update counts in real time.
- View follower/following counts on profiles.

## Explore and search
- Browse feeds, explore tags, and view collection previews.
- Open user profiles to see posts, collections, and follow status.

## Profile and account
- Update bio, location text, and avatar.
- Change password, update profile, or log out from account settings.
- To delete your account or request data export, email privacy@justzhu.com.

## Permissions used
- Camera & Photos: capture or select cat images; allow saving edited images back to your library.
- Microphone: only if you record video with sound.
- Location (while in use): only when you choose to tag a post with your current location.

## Privacy and security
- Passwords are stored only as hashes; session tokens are rotated on login.
- Media files are stored securely; database stores object keys, not raw images.
- We do not sell your data. See [Privacy Policy](https://pawparazzi.privacy.justzhu.com) for full details.

## Troubleshooting
- Cannot post: check camera/photo permissions and network connection.
- Location not attaching: ensure location permission is enabled and try again.
- Avatar not updating: wait a moment and retry; we delete old avatars when possible.
- App crashes or freezes: force quit and relaunch; if persistent, contact support with device model and iOS version.
